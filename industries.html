Anup Sinha & Associates

At Anup Sinha & Associates, we offer professional services relating to Corporate Tax (including International Tax), Transfer Pricing and Regulatory matters for domestic and global businesses. We are keen to understand the issues, objectives, opportunities, strengths and vulnerabilities of our clients. With the above understanding, we enhance value for clients by focusing on solutions which are innovative yet practical and can be implemented.Our vision is to be one of the most admired professional services firms catering to the needs of the clients globally. We maintain an uncompromising focus on quality of service by hiring and nurturing high quality professionals who have passion for excellence. The sole objective of our firm is to ensure growth of its people and growth of its clients.Founded on January 1, 2012, the firm has won the confidence of highly reputed multinational companies and Indian companies. The firm is headed by Mr. Anup Sinha, Chartered Accountant, who has vast experience in handling taxation and regulatory matters for more than twenty years.We are committed to the philosophy of mutual respect in all our dealings with clients and our people. We respect the confidentiality and privacy of our clients and comply with applicable laws, regulations and professional standards so as to maintain the appropriate degree of confidentiality and privacy.
