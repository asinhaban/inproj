We are Passionate About Helping Clients Achieve Their Goals

At Anup Sinha & Associates, we offer professional services relating to Corporate Tax (including International Tax), Transfer Pricing and Regulatory matters for domestic and global businesses. We are keen to understand the issues, objectives, opportunities, strengths and vulnerabilities of our clients. With the above understanding, we enhance value for clients by focusing on solutions which are innovative yet practical and can be implemented.

Our vision is to be one of the most admired professional services firms catering to the needs of the clients globally. We maintain an uncompromising focus on quality of service by hiring and nurturing high quality professionals who have passion for excellence. The sole objective of our firm is to ensure growth of its people and growth of its clients.

Founded on January 1, 2012, the firm has won the confidence of highly reputed multinational companies and Indian companies. The firm is headed by Mr. Anup Sinha, Chartered Accountant, who has vast experience in handling taxation and regulatory matters for more than twenty years.

We are committed to the philosophy of mutual respect in all our dealings with clients and our people. We respect the confidentiality and privacy of our clients and comply with applicable laws, regulations and professional standards so as to maintain the appropriate degree of confidentiality and privacy.

Corporate Tax is an essential aspect of doing business in India. Sound business decisions need to be backed by right tax advice. Corporate Houses have realized that progressive tax strategies and proper tax planning can reduce the tax incidence and help the companies excel globally.

 

At Anup Sinha & Associates, we offer our clients a broad range of Corporate Tax Services. Our Corporate Tax Service Team offers services in the following areas:
  • Corporate Tax Compliance
  • Representation before the Assessing Officer
  • Representation before the Commissioner of Income Tax/ Commissioner of Income Tax (Appeals)
  • Representation before the Income Tax Appellate Tribunal
  • Preparation of brief for Senior Counsels in respect of High Court matters
  • Representation before CBDT / Ministry of Finance
  • Representation before Authority of Advance Ruling
  • Representation before Settlement Commission
  • Corporate Tax Planning and Advisory Services
  • International Taxation / Cross-border Taxation
  • Expatriate Compensation Taxation

The world of Transfer Pricing has undergone a vast change in last few years. In globalised environment, complex cross-border transactions are increasing in number leading to significant increase in litigation relating to shifting of profit and erosion of tax base of the country. The Transfer Pricing policies / arrangements framed by Corporate Houses need to be backed by right advice from experts.

 

At Anup Sinha & Associates, we offer our clients a broad range of Transfer Pricing Services. Our Transfer Pricing Service Team offers services in the following areas:
  • Issuance of Accountant"s Report in respect of international transactions as well as specified domestic transactions and providing assistance in the preparation of Transfer Pricing Study Report as per the requirements of the Indian Transfer Pricing Regulation.
  • Representation before the Transfer Pricing Officer
  • Representation before the Dispute Resolution Panel
  • Representation before Commissioner of Income Tax (Appeals)
  • Representation before the Income Tax Appellate Tribunal
  • Preparation of brief for Senior Counsels in respect of High Court matters
  • Assisting Multinational Companies in framing Transfer Pricing policies.
  • Transfer Pricing Planning
  • Advance Pricing Agreement ("APA")
